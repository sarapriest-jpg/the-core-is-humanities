Tutoring

Humanities Tutoring

Online tutoring is designed to meet students where they are and help them move forward with greater confidence, independence, and academic control.

Who I support

  • Secondary students seeking stronger understanding and more confidence.
  • Students preparing for tests, exams, essays, and assessments.
  • Families looking for steady, experienced support and clear communication.

Subjects and areas of support

  • Humanities, History, Geography, and English.
  • Revision planning, essay writing, paragraph structure, and source analysis.
  • Topic review, study routines, organisation, and differentiated guidance.

My approach

Sessions are calm, purposeful, and responsive to the learner. I focus on subject knowledge, explicit guidance, and practical strategies that help students understand how to improve, not simply what to complete.

What students can gain

  • Greater confidence with content and assessment expectations.
  • Better organisation, revision habits, and essay planning.
  • A clearer sense of ownership over learning.

All tutoring is offered online. Enquiries are welcome from families and students who would like to discuss year level, subject focus, and the kind of support needed.

FAQ

Practical questions

Who is tutoring for?

Tutoring is for secondary students who need support with Humanities, History, Geography, or English, including revision, essay writing, source work, and confidence building.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes. Sessions are offered online, making it possible to support students flexibly while keeping communication clear and organised.

What makes your approach different?

My approach combines deep subject knowledge, curriculum understanding, differentiated support, and a strong pastoral philosophy. I aim to help students become more independent and self-aware learners.

Do you only work with students?

No. I also create resources and materials for teachers, and I welcome enquiries from families who want guidance before tutoring begins.

What happens before tutoring begins?

We begin with an enquiry so I can understand the year level, subject area, current concerns, and the kind of support that would be most useful.

Do you offer resources as well as tutoring?

Yes. The Core is Humanities includes teaching resources, revision support materials, planning scaffolds, and further resource development over time.
